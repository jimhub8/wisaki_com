Product Information

The mothercare sport car seat is an easy-to-fit forward-facing car seat that your child can use when they weigh between 9-18kgs ( approx. 9 months to 4 years), offering great value, comfort and safety protection.

With five seat recline positions and a 5-point harness, you'll find the mothercare Sport car seat has everything you need to keep your little one safe and protected in the vehicle. The seat has deep wide wings with side impact protection, as well as a body support cushion for younger children and a head cushion, chest and buckle pads for comfort. For snooze times, the seat reclines on its own axis and has built-in locking clips to maintain the tension of the belt.

The padded cover on the mothercare sport car seat is removable and washable, so you can keep your little one's seat clean and fresh.

For more information, please click here for our sport ISOFIX guide

With our free 'safe fit' service one of our expertly trained store staff will help you choose the right car seat for both your child and your car. They will even show you how to fit your car seat safely and securely.click here to book your car seat fitting appointment

Fabric composition: polycotton

Care Instructions: removable fabric cover - hand wash only

Safety warning: do not use on front seat with an active airbag
